Our Story

A Passion Project Born From Love

The Cozy Bean began as a dream shared between two friends, Elena and Marcus, who believed that the perfect cup of coffee could transform an ordinary moment into something extraordinary.

After years of traveling through Italy, France, and Austria, studying the art of coffee-making in centuries-old cafes, they returned to London with a vision: to create a sanctuary where time slows down, and every detail matters.

Today, we source our beans directly from sustainable farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala. Our pastries are baked fresh each morning using recipes passed down through generations. And our space? It's designed to feel like a warm embrace on a rainy London day.
